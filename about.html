About

My name is Carolina Karlsson and I am a soon-to-be Web Developer based in Stockholm, Sweden. I love visual- and UX design and I am very design-oriented in my works. My biggest passion is to build beautiful, pefect and customized web applications with carefully crafted code.

My goal is always to work towards a happy customer and in a team with good dynamic. It's my belief that you always can improve your work and the journey to improvment is by being kind, interested, inspiring and humble to both yourself, your customer and your work partners. When I'm not working I enjoy being creative by drawing and writing. I love to express myself in forms of makeup (I used to be a full time makeup artist) and clothing and you can find me in second-hand stores browsing through every item or walking around my city with my boyfriend. Part from that I'm a huge fan of video games my favorites being Fallout, Zelda and The Elder Scrolls.
